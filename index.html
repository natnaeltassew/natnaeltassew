About Me

My name is Natnael Tassew, and I am a 19-year-old Grade 12 student at Abyot Kirs Secondary School in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. I was born in Bahir Dar and grew up with two brothers. From a young age, I developed a strong interest in technology. My mother introduced me to fixing simple devices such as chargers, sockets, and phones, and later taught me how to use a computer. That moment sparked my passion for technology and motivated me to learn more. As I grew older, I actively participated in ICT activities at school and continued exploring the field during my free time. At the age of 12, I began learning fundamental computer skills, and over the years I expanded my knowledge into areas such as Python programming, Windows and Linux systems, HTML, CSS, and basic cybersecurity. My favourite subjects in school are Information Technology and Mathematics. I enjoy using my free time to learn new skills, which has helped me earn several certificates. These include three certificates in IT, AI Fundamentals, and Programming Fundamentals from Udacity, an advanced Python programming certificate from FreeCodeCamp, a theology certificate from the Ethiopian Orthodox Church, and a Grade 9 academic excellence certificate for ranking 3rd in my class.
